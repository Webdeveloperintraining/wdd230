Popular Places in Tijuana

Arco Monumental

The Monumental Arch, also known as Millennium Arch or Monumental Clock, is a famous symbol of Tijuana, located at the beginning of Revolucion Avenue in walking distance from the Mexican and US border.

Hidalgo Market

Founded in 1955, Tijuana's Mercado Hidalgo is a traditional market that offers within its aisles a wide variety of products that you will probably find nowhere else in the city.

Demographics

Population in Tijuana

The total population of Baja California is 3,769,020 inhabitants. Of these, 1,868,431 are female (49.6%) and 1,900,589 are men (50.4%).

Population by Age

The age ranges that concentrated the largest population were 20 to 24 years (176,731 inhabitants), 25 to 29 years (174,408 inhabitants), and 15 to 19 years (160,775 inhabitants). Among them they concentrated 26.6% of the total population.

Means of Transportation to Work and to School

In 2020, 39.7% of the population used to walk as the main means of transportation to work. Regarding the means of transport to go to the place of study, 64.5% of the population used bus, taxi, or similar as the main means of transportation.

Travel time

In Tijuana, the average travel time from home to work was 35.1 minutes, 81.9% of the population takes less than an hour to move, while 10.1% takes more than 1 hour to get to work.

Foreign people living in Tijuana

The population residing in the state and born in another country is 152 377 inhabitants.132 673 people were born in the United States of America, 3 016 people were born in the Republic of Honduras and 2 540 are originally from the Republic of Haiti.
